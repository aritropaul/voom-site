What is Voom?

Voom is a privacy-first screen recording app for macOS. It records your screen, camera, and microphone, transcribes everything on-device with WhisperKit, and optionally lets you share recordings via a self-hosted Cloudflare Worker. Pure Swift and SwiftUI. No Electron.

Features

Recording
Screen + Camera + Mic
Full retina HEVC capture with picture-in-picture webcam, region selection, and system audio.
Recording
Drawing Tools
Annotate your screen live during recording. Freehand, arrows, shapes, and text.
Transcription
On-Device via WhisperKit
Fully offline transcription powered by distil-large-v3. Nothing leaves your machine.
Editing
Trim, Cut, Stitch
Trim start/end, cut sections from the middle, or stitch multiple recordings together.
Editing
Filler Word Removal
Automatically detect and cut "um", "uh", "like", and other filler words from transcribed recordings.
Organization
Folders, Tags, Search
Color-coded folders and tags. Full-text search across titles and transcripts.
Sharing
Share via Link
Upload to your own Cloudflare infrastructure. Clean share page with synced transcript and captions.
Sharing
Comments, Reactions, Password
Timestamped comments, emoji reactions, optional password protection, and CTA buttons.

Share Page

Each shared recording gets a minimal dark page with video playback, live captions, a clickable synced transcript, emoji reactions at timestamps, and timestamped comments. Optional password protection and CTA button overlays. Links auto-expire after 30 days.

Cloud Sharing is Free

Voom's sharing infrastructure runs entirely within Cloudflare's free tier. No credit card required.

Service Purpose Free Tier
Workers API + share page 100,000 requests/day
R2 Video storage 10 GB, zero egress
D1 Metadata & transcripts 5 GB, 5M reads/day
Cron Daily cleanup Included

Set up in under a minute with the in-app self-host wizard, or deploy manually with wrangler.

Getting Started

Download the latest release, or build from source: 

git clone https://github.com/aritropaul/voom.git
cd voom/Voom
open Voom.xcodeproj

Requires macOS 15+. Cloud sharing is optional — Voom works entirely offline.
